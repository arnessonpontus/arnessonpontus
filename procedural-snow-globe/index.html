Procedural Snow Globe

A Procedural Snow Globe created in THREE.js

A procedural snow globe made for the course TNM084 - Procedural Images. The idea was to create a webb based application with interactive controls of the environment. The result is a THREE.js procedural sphere with snow flakes falling down to the ground. Storm can also be turned on causing the snow flakes swirl around in the globe.

The storm is created using Flow noise based on Simplex noise to create the wind looking swirls. The ground can be adjusted in both high and low frequency noise, with the addition of increasing snow level depending on the amount of snow falling to the ground.
