Bibliuteket

An Android and IOS application for used school literature

Bibliuteket is an application with the aim of helping students sell and buy used course literature. The application was started as a project for the course TNM040 - Kommunikation och användargränssnitt. After a while the idea to launch the app came to life again and we started to spend time on it outside of school.

Now the application can be found on both App Store and Google Play.
